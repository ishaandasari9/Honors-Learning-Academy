Good questions

What parents usually ask.

Is it really free?
Yes. Honors Learning Academy is a student-founded nonprofit pursuing 501(c)(3) status. There is no fee, no membership, and no paid upgrade. Tutoring is a charitable service.
Who are the tutors?
High-achieving high-school and college students we know and approve before they tutor. Many recently took the same classes, so they teach the way the material actually clicks. We vet every tutor before a first session.
Online, in person, or both?
Both. Online sessions work anywhere. In-person sessions happen around Waxhaw and Marvin, North Carolina. You tell us your preference on the request form.
What grades and subjects do you cover?
Kindergarten through 12th grade. Core subjects are math, science, English and writing, and social studies. We also run specialized tracks: computer science, business, pre-med, and pre-law (public speaking and legal literacy).
How soon will we hear back?
Usually within a few days. We email the parent or guardian with a tutor match and a couple of times that could work.
How do you handle our information?
We collect only what we need to make a match and reach you, and we don't sell or share it. If you ever want your information removed, email us and we'll take care of it.
