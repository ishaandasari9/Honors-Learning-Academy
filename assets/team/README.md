# Team photos

Drop portrait photos here, then set the matching `photo` filename on an entry in `window.HLA_TEAM` in `js/config.js`.

## Spec

- **Size:** 1200 × 1500 px (4:5 portrait)
- **Format:** JPEG or WebP
- **Weight:** under 120KB after compression
- **Framing:** faces framed consistently, similar lighting, plain or softly blurred background
- **Filename:** `firstname.jpg` (or `.webp`), matched to the `photo` field in `HLA_TEAM`

Only include someone who has agreed to appear. If they have not, leave `photo` empty and the site shows a monogram tile instead.
