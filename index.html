Why families trust us

Careful with your kid, honest about the rest.

Tutor standards

  • Approved before matched. No tutor meets a student until we have reviewed and approved their application.

  • They teach only what they know cold. Tutors list the subjects they are confident in and we match to that list.

  • Every session goes on the record. Date, subject, and hours, logged in the portal.

  • We collect only what we need. Names and an email to make the match, nothing sold, nothing shared.

  • A real person answers. Email a founder and you get a reply.
